FAQ


What is the Home page?
The Home page allows the teacher to give or revoke permissions to specific applications on a student's OS container.

What is the Messaging page?
The Messaging page gives teachers the ability to send personalized messages to a student with a unique userid

What is the Students page?
This page lets teachers view screenshots of every student's screen.
Additionally, teachers can click on the screenshot to view a live feed from the student's device, or kill their session.

What is the Connect page?
The Connect page allows teachers and students to connect to a Windows 10 or Linux Ununtu operating system.
These are currently the only operating systems supported by StreamingOS at this time.
